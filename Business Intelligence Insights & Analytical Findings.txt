1. High-Dimensional Complexity Identified

Initial analysis revealed significant multicollinearity among chemical attributes such as phenols, flavanoids, and color intensity. This redundancy inflated analytical noise and reduced interpretability.

BI Insight:

Raw feature analysis obscures true structure; dimensionality reduction is essential for strategic clarity.

2. PCA as a Strategic Information Filter

PCA reduced 13 chemical attributes into 2 principal components, retaining approximately 70% of total variance. These components represent:

PC1: Overall richness & intensity (phenols, alcohol, proline)

PC2: Balance & structural characteristics (hue, dilution)

BI Insight:

PCA reframes complex chemistry into business-friendly dimensions: intensity and balance.
